Security Policy

Last updated: 2024 · Lenox's Marketing Hub

Password Security

All passwords are hashed using SHA-256 with a unique salt per user. Passwords are never stored in plain text and cannot be read by anyone, including the app owner.

Login Protection

Login attempts are rate-limited to 5 tries per 15 minutes per account. This prevents brute-force attacks on user accounts.

Session Tokens

Sessions use cryptographically random tokens. Sessions expire after 7 days and auto-logout occurs after 30 minutes of inactivity.

Input Sanitization

All user inputs are sanitized to prevent Cross-Site Scripting (XSS) attacks. Phone numbers and emails are validated before storage.

Content Security Policy

A strict CSP prevents unauthorized scripts, frames, and external resources from loading. X-Frame-Options is set to DENY to prevent clickjacking.

Data Storage

User data is stored locally on your device only. No data is sent to external servers. You can delete your data anytime by clearing your browser storage.

Report a Vulnerability

If you discover a security issue, please contact us via WhatsApp or email before publicly disclosing it. We take all reports seriously.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024

What we collect: Name, email, phone number, and location when you register.

How we use it: Only to identify your account and display your listings. We do not sell your data.

Where it's stored: All data is stored locally on your own device using browser storage. No external servers.

Your rights: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by clearing your browser/app storage.

Cookies: We do not use tracking cookies. Only essential session data is stored.

Terms of Use

Last updated: 2024

1. Acceptable Use: Users must not post illegal, fraudulent, or misleading listings. All items must be legal to sell in Ghana.

2. Accounts: You are responsible for keeping your account secure. Do not share your password with anyone.

3. Transactions: Lenox's Marketing Hub is a listing platform only. We are not responsible for transactions between buyers and sellers.

4. Safety: Always meet in a public place when exchanging goods. Use MoMo for secure payments.

5. Prohibited Items: Weapons, drugs, counterfeit goods, and stolen items are strictly prohibited.

6. Violations: Accounts found violating these terms will be suspended immediately.
